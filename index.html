MathWorks interview experience: why I did not clear

My MathWorks experience taught me that understanding concepts is not enough; speed, communication, and clarity under pressure are equally important. I missed consistency in translating thought process into concise answers, and I under-invested in timed practice for edge-case heavy DSA rounds.

What changed after that: I now run weekly mock interviews, explain solutions out loud, revise core CS fundamentals with short notes, and benchmark progress using timed question sets.
